[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel your breath upon my skin tonight
Lost inside the embers of this fading light
Every word you whisper sets my soul on fire
Pulling me deeper into sweet desire

[Verse 1]
We left the dusty roads and the world behind
In this quiet room, just the ties that bind
Your hands upon my chest and a slow, soft sway
We have nowhere to be till the break of day

[Pre-Chorus]
The shadows are dancing across your face
I am caught in the heat of your sweet embrace
I am leaning in closer, tasting the air
Lost in the passion that we both share

[Chorus]
Oh, the echoes of your voice in the quiet of the room
Chasing away the lonely, sweeping out the gloom
I want to hold you closer than I ever did before
Leaving our shadows tangled up upon the floor

[Post-Chorus]
Echoes of your voice, echoing so sweet
Making every piece of my heavy heart complete

[Verse 2]
The coffee is forgotten, only we remain
A soft burning fire that we cannot contain
My fingers trace the curves right along your spine
Feeling the shiver as your skin meets mine

[Bridge]
Every heartbeat pulls us closer to the edge
Falling deep into the vows that we pledge
You whisper Saeed, and the fears fade away
A quiet devotion we want to make stay
Our souls intertwine in the middle of the night
Holding onto this fire until the morning light
